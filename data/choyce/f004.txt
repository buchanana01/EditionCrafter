To make apricock Biscakes
take your. apricocks before they
be too ripe pare them & slice
them in to a silver Cann
putt your. Cann in to a skillet
of watter let them Boyle till
you can power out the Juice
as you doe for Cleere cakes
then putt in your. Cann in to the
watter againe letting them boyle
till they ar very tender
then Pulp them through a
sive, and take to Euery
ownce of this pulp 2 ownces
