Choyce receits collected ^out of the book of Receits, of the Lady Vere Wilkinson line divider Susana Hixon her booke January: 28. begun to be written by the Right Honorable the Lady Anne Carr January. 28. 1673/4 2 To make apricock Biscakes take your. apricocks before they be too ripe pare them & slice them in to a silver Cann putt your. Cann in to a skillet of watter let them Boyle till you can power out the Juice as you doe for Cleere cakes then putt in your. Cann in to the watter againe letting them boyle till they ar very tender then Pulp them through a sive, and take to Euery ownce of this pulp 2 ownces
